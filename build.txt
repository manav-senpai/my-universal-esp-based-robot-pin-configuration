PROJECT ARCEE / UNIVERSAL ESP32 ROBOT PINOUT (STANDARD 1)
=========================================================
Author: Manav Ahire
Date: March 2026
Note: Common Ground (GND) must be shared across all components!
=========================================================

[1] POWER BLOCK (Heavy Duty Screw Terminals)
---------------------------------------------------------
Terminal 1: 12V  (Direct from BMS / 18650s)
Terminal 2: 5V   (From Buck Converter)
Terminal 3: 3.3V (From ESP32 or Step-down)
Terminal 4: GND  (Common Earth)

[2] THE BASE BUILD (Hardwired Core)
---------------------------------------------------------
Left Motor Driver (BTS7960):
- RPWM (Forward): GPIO 32
- LPWM (Reverse): GPIO 33

Right Motor Driver (BTS7960):
- RPWM (Forward): GPIO 27
- LPWM (Reverse): GPIO 14

Core Controls & Lighting:
- Status LED 1 (System OK): GPIO 16
- Status LED 2 (ESP-NOW):   GPIO 17
- Headlights Trigger (NPN): GPIO 18
- Taillights Trigger (NPN): GPIO 19
- Mode Switch Button:       GPIO 13 (Input w/ Pullup)

[3] FRONT EXPANSION (Audio / Voice)
---------------------------------------------------------
Exposed Female Headers at the front of the chassis.
- Speaker Left Channel (DAC 1):  GPIO 25
- Speaker Right Channel (DAC 2): GPIO 26
*Note: Plug PAM8403 Amp directly here.*

[4] REAR EXPANSION BAY (10-Pin Data Block)
---------------------------------------------------------
Exposed Female Headers at the back for attachments.
(Arranged in a 5x2 grid or single line as needed)

-- I2C Communication (For OLEDs, Gyros, etc.) --
- SDA: GPIO 21
- SCL: GPIO 22

-- General Output/PWM (For Servos, Arms, Lights) --
- IO A: GPIO 4
- IO B: GPIO 5
- IO C: GPIO 23
- IO D: GPIO 15 (Note: Boot strapping pin, keep floating at startup)

-- Input Only (For IR, Ultrasonics, Analog Sensors) --
- IN W: GPIO 34
- IN X: GPIO 35
- IN Y: GPIO 36 (VP)
- IN Z: GPIO 39 (VN)
